Meet Dr. Shiny Adhikary

General Physician in Kolkata

About

Dr. Shiny Adhikary is a qualified General Physician practicing in Kolkata, known for her calm approach and patient-centered healthcare. As a trusted primary care doctor with over four years of clinical experience and 2,000+ patients treated, she believes in building lasting relationships with her patients through attentive listening and evidence-based medicine focused on accurate diagnosis and holistic wellness.

Education & Training

Dr. Adhikary completed her MBBS degree from a prestigious Government Medical College with academic excellence. She pursued residency training in the Neuromedicine Department where she developed expertise in managing conditions ranging from chronic headaches, stroke, migraine, neuropathic pain, seizure disorders, and complex medical cases requiring meticulous diagnostic skills and compassionate patient management.

Commitment to Patient Care

She values clear, empathetic communication and takes time to understand each patient's concerns, creating personalized treatment plans tailored to individual needs. Known as a reliable family doctor in Kolkata, Dr. Adhikary is especially appreciated for her gentle approach, thorough follow-ups, and ensuring patients and their families feel supported and informed at every step of their healthcare journey.

Professional Interests

Beyond her clinic, Dr. Adhikary is an advocate for Preventive Health and Women's Wellness. She actively promotes lifestyle management to prevent chronic diseases before they start. She is committed to delivering modern, reliable, and patient-centered healthcare that supports long-term wellness and empower families with the knowledge they need to lead healthier, happier lives.

Treatments

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment from Trusted General Physician in Kolkata for acute and chronic diseases.

I focus on accurate diagnosis and treatment with minimal medicine for rapid recovery to get you back to health.

Hypertension (High BP)

Complete blood pressure management including cardiovascular risk evaluation, kidney assessment (CKD screening), ECG interpretation, and personalised medication to prevent stroke and heart complications.

Diabetes Management

Holistic Type 2 Diabetes care including blood sugar profiling, HbA1c monitoring, insulin therapy planning, and dietary counselling to prevent complications involving eyes, nerves, and feet.

Cardiac & Stroke Prevention

Evaluation of chest pain, palpitations, and breathlessness using ECG and risk profiling. Includes cholesterol management and preventive therapy for patients at risk of heart attack or stroke.

Joint & Back Pain

Medical management for arthritis, back pain, and muscle strain. Treatment includes pain control protocols, ergonomic corrections, and physiotherapy guidance to restore mobility.

Asthma & Respiratory Care

Treatment for Asthma, COPD, and Bronchitis. Services include inhaler technique optimisation, allergy control, and oxygen assessment to reduce breathing difficulties.

Viral Fever & Infections

Rapid diagnosis for seasonal flu, dengue, typhoid, and viral fevers. Includes hydration planning and monitoring to prevent complications like pneumonia or sinusitis.
